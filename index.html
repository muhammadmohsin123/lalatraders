Who We Are

Trusted Partner in Construction

Lala Arif Traders (SMC-Private) Limited is a professionally managed construction material supply and logistics company, headquartered in Muzaffargarh, Punjab. Incorporated in 2017, we have actively supported infrastructure and development projects across Pakistan.

Our proprietor, Muhammad Arif, has built strong relationships with Pakistan's top cement, steel, and materials manufacturers — making us the go-to partner for mega projects, road construction, housing schemes, and power & energy developments.

From a single bag of cement to full-scale project supply chains, we deliver with the same commitment to quality, professionalism, and on-time performance.
