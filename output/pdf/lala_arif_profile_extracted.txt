ARIF TRADERS
(SMC-PRIVATE) LIMITED
BRIEF COMPANY INTRODUCTION
Arif Traders (SMC-Private) Limited is a professionally managed construction material
supply and logistics company. Since its incorporation in 2017, the company has been
actively supporting infrastructure and development projects across Pakistan by providing
quality materials and reliable transportation services under one roof.
VISION
To become a trusted national partner in construction and infrastructure development
through quality products, dependable services, and long-term business relationships.
MISSION
To contribute to Pakistan's infrastructure growth by supplying high-quality construction
materials and efficient logistics solutions for roads, highways, canals, buildings,
housing schemes, and power & energy projects.
CORE VALUES
Quality and Reliability Customer Satisfaction Professional Integrity
Timely Delivery Long-Term Partnerships
PRODUCTS & SERVICES
Arif Traders (SMC-Private) Limited deals in a comprehensive range of construction
and infrastructure materials, supported by a strong nationwide transportation setup.
TRANSPORTATION & LOGISTICS
The company operates a wide transportation network across Pakistan, ensuring
timely delivery of construction materials to project sites nationwide. Our logistics
capability enables us to support small-scale developments as well as large
infrastructure and mega projects.
CONCLUSION
With a strong commitment to quality, professionalism, and customer satisfaction,
Arif Traders (SMC-Private) Limited continues to support Pakistan's growing
infrastructure needs by delivering reliable materials and services for
sustainable development.

FAUJI CEMENT COMPANY LIMITED
FAUJI CEMENT INTRODUCTION
A longtime leader in the cement manufacturing industry, Fauji Cement
Company, headquartered in Rawalpindi, operates a cement plant at Nhang
Bahtar, Tehsil Fateh Jang, District Attock in the province of Punjab. The
Company has a strong and longstanding tradition of services, reliability,
and quality that reaches back more than 22 years. Sponsored by Fauji
Foundation, the Company was incorporated in Rawalpindi in 1992.
Fauji Cement is operating two lines of Cement Plants, one each from
FLS Denmark & TKIS (Thyssen Krupplndustrial Solutions) Germany. The
Plants are well reowned for their high efficiencies, best quality production
and are well maintained with annual total production capacity of 3.43 million
tons of cement. FAUJI Cement enjoys the reputation of being the Best Quality
Cement in the country and is preferred in the construction of Mega Projects
like Dams, Bridges, Highways & Motorways, Housing Societies and a myriad
of other structures needing consistent setting time / quality and high strength,
fundamental to Pakistan’s economic vitality and quality of life

MUGHAL STEEL COMPANY LIMITED
MUGHAL STEEL INTRODUCTION
Mughal Steel (Mughal Iron & Steel Industries Ltd.) is a major
Pakistani steel manufacturer, established in the 1950s, known for
its advanced technology in producing high-quality rebar, girders,
and structural steel, playing a key role in Pakistan's construction
sector with a focus on quality, innovation, and ethical practices,
growing from traders to a listed public company.
Origins & Growth: Started as Mughal Traders in the 1950s, importing
steel, and evolved into a major producer, incorporating
in 2008 and becoming publicly listed in 2015. Specializes
in construction materials like high-strength deformed bars
(rebar), girder steel, and structural sections. Employs modern techniques,
including advanced melting and rolling processes with online
monitoring, aiming for efficiency and quality. A leading player in
Pakistan's steel industry, contributing significantly to the
national infrastructure, with expansions to increase capacity.
